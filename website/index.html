Welcome to Cub Scout Pack 342

Cub Scout Pack 342 strives to offer a values-based youth development program that focuses on character building, service to the community and appreciation for the outdoors. We are a family and volunteer-run organization that focuses on personal development through fun, adventure, achievement and hands-on learning via structured activities.
 
Join us as we establish a strong foundation that will lead to our sons becoming strong, independent, good and moral leaders for the next generation.
